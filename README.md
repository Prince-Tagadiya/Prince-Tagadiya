## 🧬 The Engineer Behind the Circuits

I'm a second-year ICT engineering student who lives at the intersection of **analog electronics, embedded systems, IoT, and full-stack software**. While most people reach for a microcontroller to solve a problem, I ask first: *"Can logic gates and transistors do this instead?"* — that mindset earned a hardware safety helmet a national hackathon win with **zero microcontrollers on board**.

I lead every team I'm part of — from SSIP-funded drone research to AI-powered hiring platforms — and I've been recognized nationally for it: **Runner-up among 900+ participants at BGI Hackathon 2026**, **1st place at Technovation Hackathon 2026**, and a project **published in Electronics For You (EFY)**, India's leading electronics magazine.
